Leadership Scholar's Program

The UNCA Leadership Scholars Program (LSP) is a premier four-year student leadership development program at UNC Asheville. This cohort-based program of ten scholars each year is designed to help students maximize their leadership skills through self-discovery, experiential learning, and career readiness. During their four years in the program, scholars critically examine leadership systems and structures in order to take action on issues that matter to them. Scholars engage with a variety of thought leaders across disciplines and build deep connections with fellow UNCA students, faculty, staff, and alumni. The program requires bi-weekly participation for a variety of learning opportunities. Scholars are expected to commit at least two hours per week to program components.
